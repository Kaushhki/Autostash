How it works

Three moves,
every single day

AutoStash watches your checking account the way a careful friend would: it never sweeps a dollar that a bill might need first. What's left over gets pushed straight at your most expensive debt.

The math is the same each day — measure what's safe, rank the debt, sweep and allocate. No manual transfers, no guesswork about which card to pay down first.

This dashboard can run entirely offline in your browser, or connect to the real Django API below for persisted, database-backed calculations.

01

Measure safe cash

Checking balance, minus your safety buffer, minus anything due in the next 5 days. Capped at 25% of the balance so no single sweep goes too far.

02

Rank the debt

Cards are sorted highest-APR first. That's the avalanche method — it minimizes total interest paid versus paying off small balances first.

03

Sweep & allocate

The safe amount is pulled and dropped onto the top card. Overflow past that card's balance cascades to the next one.
